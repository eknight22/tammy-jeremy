This Thanksgiving...

I’m thankful for stuffing and cranberry jam,
But mostly for Jeremy and my girl Tam.

It’s a funny arrangement, this friendship we share:
I handle the hardware; you handle the care.

I help with your glitches and cracked LCDs,
You move me to fight this fucking disease.

I’m bad at the phone (yeah, I know it’s a crime),
Yet you’ve stuck with me for a very long time.

Through all the chaos, one thing is true:
I’m deeply thankful I wound up with you.

If lives can be said to have VIPs,
Tammy and Jerms, you are that for me.

I love you both…

Eric Papa
